Why Use Digital Business Card ?



Easy To Share

You can easily share digital business cards with your clients and people. All you need to do is to send the card to them via email, social media, text[SMS], and other ways.

Attach Media

A significant advantage is that businesses can attach a variety of media with digital cards. They can add videos, links to their surveys, subscription lists, sign up forms, etc. This is useful in many ways. You promote your business by sharing video etc. A client receiving the card has a good impression of your business due to a variety of media included.

Stand Out

Since not everyone is using digital business cards, you can easily impress clients and stand out with these cards. While others are still sticking to conventional paper cards, just because of the digital cards, your business looks different.

Storage of Information

It is a convenient way to store as much information as you want in one place. Unlike the paper cards, there is no lack of space here. So, you can include your URLs, website addresses, social media handles, fax number, phone number, and many more in the card.

Easy to Update

A problem with the paper cards is that whenever you update information, you need to redesign and reprint the cards. That makes them costly for small businesses. But that is not the issue with the digital counterparts. A digital business card design lets you automatically update the information. You do not have to reprint a new set of cards and no need to redesign either.

Easy To Customize

Customizing of the paper cards is a difficult task as you have to incorporate new elements of colors etc. Then the card has to go again through the printing process. But digital business cards can be quickly customized to a brand with the help of a business card maker software. Just choose the contact details, colors, typeface, etc., and you have the new card. There is no need to go through the time-consuming designing and printing process.

Cost Effective

The digital cards do not require you to spend a lot of money unlike paper cards on printing and designing. In fact, the cost of printing of cards on paper is very high for many businesses. But the digital cards are free to have as there is no printing required. You will spend some money only on designing the card.

Creates an Ever-Lasting Impression

The modern cards are designed to impress clients. They have a planned use of color, typeface, space, logo, image etc. These elements combine to make the desired impact. The impression remains at the back of the clients’ mind and creates some perception about a brand. Digital business cards, like their paper counterparts, also make a great lasting impression.

Eco-Friendly

Digital business cards eliminate the need to cut tree for the paper. This helps in keeping the environment safe and secure for a healthy living.
